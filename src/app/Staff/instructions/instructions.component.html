Introduction:


Khanzo Play is a multifaceted mobile application developed by Khanzo Media Company. Our app offers a subscription-based model, providing users with a range of exciting features and services. With Khanzo Play, users can access exclusive shopping discount coupons and deals, watch free video content, and engage in affiliate marketing, all within a single app. Here's an in-depth overview of the app's main functionalities and pages:


App Features and Uses:


Subscription Model:


Users can subscribe to Khanzo Play for one year at a cost of Rs. 242, which includes all applicable GST. 

This subscription is essential for accessing exclusive shopping discount coupons and deals.


Signup and Verification:


Users need to sign up using their email address, which helps create a personalized experience.

After signing up, a verification link is sent to the user's email for authentication.

Once the email is verified, users can log in and provide basic personal information.


Payment with Referral and Credit Codes:


Payment for the subscription requires the use of a referral code and a credit code, both of which are provided by other users.

These codes grant access to the app's features, and they are mandatory for payment.


3-Day Free Trial:


To give new users a taste of what Khanzo Play offers, we provide a generous 3-day free trial of the subscription.


Unique Referral Codes:


Each user is assigned a unique referral code that they can share with others.

Sharing your referral code can lead to valuable rewards and earnings.


User Levels and Earnings:


Users start at level A and can progress to higher levels by successfully referring others to the app.

As users advance through levels, they earn level-up income, creating an opportunity to maximize earnings.

The minimum withdrawal amount is 500Rs, and there is a 15% service charge for withdrawals.

Users become eligible for withdrawal requests when their wallet reaches the maximum amount of 500Rs and they have successfully completed the daily task by watching videos.


Pages:


Home Page:


The home page offers a diverse range of free video content across various categories.

To support the app's sustainability, non-intrusive ad banners are displayed on the home page, ensuring a quality viewing experience.


Daily Task Page:


This page encourages users to complete daily tasks, such as watching videos.

For each video watched, users receive valuable coupons, enhancing the overall experience.

Upon completing daily tasks, users are rewarded with 2 empty coupons that can be used for future discounts.


Wallet Page:


In the wallet, users have the opportunity to refer the app to others, earning 50Rs for each successful referral.

Initially, users can share their referral code with the first two people, who can then use this code during the payment process.

Users can refer an unlimited number of individuals to the app, creating a substantial opportunity for earnings.

As users successfully refer others, they advance through levels, beginning at level A and moving toward level Z, with each level upgrade offering an opportunity for increased income.


Reward Page:


Users can redeem the coupons they earn by completing daily tasks. These redeemed coupons are listed in the "Redeemed Coupons" section.

Additionally, the app provides 2 empty coupons in the "Available Coupons" section, allowing users to select new coupons from a wide range of categories.


Shopping Page:


Khanzo Play offers users two distinct shopping options:

Online Shop: This section contains affiliate links to various e-commerce websites, enabling users to access a variety of products and deals.

Offline Shop: In this section, users can find information about different types of physical stores, including contact details and maps for convenience. 

Please note that this section serves as a directory and does not facilitate direct shopping or shipping through the app.


Menu Page:


The menu page offers several key sections:


User Personal Details: Users can edit their personal information as needed.

Levels: Here, users can view their current level and explore the level-up chart.

Refer to Refer Level Income Chart: This section provides users with a clear overview of the income opportunities associated with different levels.

About Khanzo Play: Users can access detailed information about the app and its features.


It is important to note that user details may be shared with third parties for marketing purposes as stated in the app's privacy policy
